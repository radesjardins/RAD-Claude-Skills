Run a quick competitive SEO check against: **$ARGUMENTS**

Use the `competitor-intelligence` skill to perform:

1. **Content Comparison**: What topics do they cover? What formats do they use? What content gaps exist?
2. **Technical Quick-Check**: Page speed, schema markup, URL structure, mobile-friendliness
3. **SERP Feature Scan**: Who owns featured snippets, FAQ results, and "People Also Ask" for shared keywords?
4. **AI Visibility Check**: Are they being recommended by AI search engines? Which ones? Why?
5. **Opportunity Identification**: Top 5 exploitable gaps where you can outperform them

Output a concise comparison table and prioritized action list.
