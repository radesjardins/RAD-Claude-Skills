# Link Building Tactics Encyclopedia

Complete reference of link building strategies sourced from Moz, Backlinko, and Google Search Central best practices.

## High-Impact Tactics

### The Skyscraper Technique
1. Find top-performing content in your industry that has earned many links
2. Create "10x content" — significantly more comprehensive, up-to-date, or visually appealing
3. Reach out to sites linking to the original piece to promote your superior version
**Best for:** Informational content in competitive niches

### Broken Link Building
1. Search for resource pages or industry blogs
2. Use tools (Check My Links, Ahrefs) to find dead links (404 errors)
3. Find all sites linking to the broken resource
4. Reach out to notify about the dead link, offer your content as replacement
**Best for:** Earning links from established resource pages

### The Moving Man Method
1. Find outdated, rebranded, or moved resources (businesses that changed names/shut down)
2. Use backlink analysis to find sites still linking to old URLs
3. Email site owners about their outdated link, suggest your updated content
**Best for:** Low-competition link opportunities with high conversion rates

### Resource Page Link Building
1. Find curated link pages: `Keyword inurl:resources` or `Keyword intitle:links`
2. Identify where your content would be a perfect fit
3. Pitch your resource to the page manager
4. Combine with broken link building for higher conversion (point out their dead links)
**Best for:** Niche authority building

### Digital PR & Original Research
1. Conduct original research, surveys, or industry studies
2. Publish unique data that journalists and bloggers need
3. Reach out to publications who need statistics for their stories
**Best for:** High-authority links from news sites and publications

### HARO / Media Requests
1. Use platforms: Featured, Connectively (formerly HARO), Qwoted, #journorequest
2. Find journalists asking for expert insights
3. Submit brief, valuable quotes or answers
4. Publication features your bio and backlink
**Best for:** Earning links from high-DA news and magazine sites

## Medium-Impact Tactics

### Guest Posting (Strategic)
- Partner with contextually aligned publishers where your audience exists
- Write highly valuable content solving problems for their audience
- Include "helpful resources" section in body (not just bio link) — the "Upside Down" technique
**Best for:** Building topical authority and referral traffic

### Link Bait / Linkable Assets
Create content engineered to attract links:
- Comprehensive ultimate guides
- Controversial or contrarian opinion pieces
- Original data, surveys, and statistics
- Visual assets (infographics, interactive tools)
- Free tools and calculators
**Best for:** Passive, ongoing link acquisition

### Branded Strategies
1. Develop a unique framework or methodology
2. Give it a sticky, memorable name (e.g., "The Briefcase Technique")
3. Publish a detailed case study proving it works
4. Bloggers and AI models cite and link to named entities
**Best for:** Long-term authority building and AEO

### Link Reclamation (Unlinked Mentions)
1. Set up alerts (Mention, Semrush Brand Monitoring) for brand mentions
2. Find positive mentions without links
3. Email author thanking them, ask to add a link
4. Also reclaim links for unauthorized image usage
**Best for:** Easy wins with high conversion rate

### Competitor Link Gap Analysis
1. Identify sites linking to competitors but not you
2. Analyze how competitors earned those links
3. Reach out with your own superior content
**Best for:** Strategic targeting of proven link sources

### Link Roundups
1. Find blogs publishing daily/weekly/monthly content roundups: `Keyword + "link roundup"`
2. Pitch your best content for inclusion
**Best for:** Consistent, recurring link opportunities

## Lower-Effort Tactics

### Affiliate Programs
- Launch affiliate program to incentivize reviews and "best tools" roundups
- Equip affiliates with talking points and screenshots
**Best for:** Scalable mentions across many sites

### Podcasting
- Pitch as expert guest on industry podcasts
- Earn backlink in episode show notes
**Best for:** Authority building plus link acquisition

### Testimonials
- Send testimonials to companies whose products you use
- They publish with your name and backlink
**Best for:** Easy, no-outreach-required links

### Blogger Reviews
- Offer product/software to bloggers for free
- Don't demand link/review — let them decide
**Best for:** Authentic, editorial-quality links

### Content Transformation
- Convert successful content to new formats (blog → video, infographic, SlideShare)
- Update stale evergreen content with fresh data
- Notify previous linkers about updates
**Best for:** Maximizing ROI from existing content

### Local Community Links
- Sponsor local events, scholarships, donations
- Join business associations
- Run local competitions
**Best for:** Local SEO link building

### Wikipedia Dead Link Technique
1. Search: `site:wikipedia.org "keyword" intext:"dead link"`
2. Find Wikipedia entries with broken references
3. Use backlink tool to find all sites linking to the dead resource
4. Reach out to those sites (not Wikipedia directly)
**Best for:** Finding high-quality link prospects from authoritative sources

### Giveaway Contests
- Give away products/software
- Incentivize sharing for entries
**Best for:** Viral link acquisition campaigns

## The 50/50 Rule

Spend 50% of time creating link-worthy content and 50% actively promoting it. "Build it and they will come" does not work in SEO. Link building is an active endeavor.

## LLM Co-Citation Strategy (AEO-Specific)

Earn unlinked brand mentions on third-party sites, Reddit, Quora, and industry publications. AI models look for "co-citations" — your brand mentioned alongside established industry leaders — to learn you are a trusted entity, even without a clickable link.

**Tactics:**
- Publish comparison content mentioning your brand alongside leaders
- Engage helpfully in Reddit/Quora threads about your industry
- Earn mentions in roundup articles with recognized players
- Get listed in same "best of" lists as established brands
