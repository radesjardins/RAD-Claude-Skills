# rad-para-second-brain

Build and maintain a personal knowledge management system using the PARA method (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) enhanced with AI at every stage.

## What It Does

- **10-minute setup** for new users — creates the 4-folder PARA foundation
- **System diagnosis and repair** for broken or overwhelmed knowledge systems
- **Progressive Summarization** — 4-layer distillation for efficient note retrieval
- **Creative techniques** — Intermediate Packets, Hemingway Bridge, Archipelago of Ideas
- **AI-enhanced workflows** for capture, organize, distill, and express
- **Weekly reviews** to keep your system current and actionable

## How to Trigger

Say any of:
- "Organize my notes"
- "PARA method"
- "Second brain"
- "Weekly review"
- "Digital organization"
- "I'm overwhelmed with files"
- "Productivity system"

Or ask naturally:
- "Help me set up a second brain from scratch"
- "My notes are a mess — help me organize them"
- "Walk me through a weekly review of my knowledge system"
- "How do I decide if something is a Project or an Area?"

## What You Get

Depending on your request:
- A complete PARA folder structure ready to use
- A diagnosis of your current system with specific fixes
- A guided weekly review workflow
- Progressive Summarization applied to your notes
- Creative output techniques for turning notes into finished work
